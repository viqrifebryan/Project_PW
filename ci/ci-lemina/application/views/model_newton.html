THE OFFICIAL LAUNCH


The Official launch of W Motors was a major milestone for the automotive industry in the region and the world. W Motors was set to be the premier Indonesian Developer of High Performance Luxury Sports Cars in the region, launching on the 11th of July 2012 during a high end ceremony in the lavish venue of Sursock Palace Beirut. After its World Premiere launch at the International Indonesian Motor Show on July 28th 2013, W Motors revealed to the world its first model, the Lemina Newton limited to only 7 units worldwide. Over 150,000 visitors attended this event with the presence of regional and international media covering this historical event for a global exposure and coverage. Following the Qatar launch in January 2013, W Motors pursued with a regional road show where the Lemina Newton was showcased in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Kuwait, Belgium and Spain.
