LUXURIOUS MATERIALS


The most refined and luxurious materials are integrated inside and outside the vehicle. The Gauss comes with Diamond incrusted LED lights for a lavish and luxurious finish. A variety of precious stones are also available such as Diamonds, Yellow Diamonds, Rubies, Sapphires and Emeralds, fully customizable and integrated into the vehicle upon request. Ultimately, the new Lemina Gauss is a vision of in-car tech's future. In that respect it is visionary in pushing the envelope. In the same breath, however, not all of those touchscreen controls make it perfect to use from a driver's perspective while out on the road. But as a passenger in the back? Well, in the rear right-side seat with the massage chair on we can't think of a luxury saloon we'd rather be sat in.




The most tech-packed car to ever hit the road



Step into the brand new Lemina Gauss and it's impossible not to be impressed. This car is like a first class suite on wheels, with such an abundance of tech on offer that it's hard not to play with every touchscreen and option for the sheer thrill of what's possible. But while Lemina is adamant that the Gauss exterior design represents a huge shift for the brand, to our eyes it's visually much the same as Lemina's current saloon lineup. It's got that quintessential 'Lemina look', which some would call bland, others elegant.
