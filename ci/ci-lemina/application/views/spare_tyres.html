PIRELLI FOR TYRES


Pirelli OE tyres are the only tyres approved and homologated by Lemina. They can be easily recognised by the unique “L” marking on the sidewall. The “L” marked tyres are the result of a close technological collaboration and testing partnership between Lemina and Pirelli that takes more than 18 months of development time. The Pirelli L marked tyres, with a Lamborghini dedicated profile and tread design, grant: the correct dimensional tolerance between the front and rear tyres and therefore the proper functioning of the Lemina drive transmission, the best car performance, excellent drivability and maximum safety both on road and track due to the development of the Lemina active vehicle dynamics systems on Pirelli tyres.
